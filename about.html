About Me

I bridge the gap between business goals and human needs, using curiosity to discover valuable insights while creating extensively thought-out experiences.

Hi, my name is Lerin! I'm a designer currently based in Philadelphia, PA. I've worked on a broad scope of projects in both graphic and UX/UI design. With a visual communication degree, I've thrived in design & development for 8 years, successfully integrating technical expertise and creativity while always considering the bigger picture. I'm also certified in Frontend Development for Designers allowing me to bridge the gap between design and implementation.

Beyond my design work, I hold a strong passion for the game of basketball, which is a constant source of inspiration. I'm also a fan of activities like running and hiking that keep me energized and focused. And, of course, life wouldn't be complete without spending quality time with my wife and our pup, Binnie.

If you have a project in mind, or a specific skill that you're looking for or simply want to say hi, feel free to reach out!
